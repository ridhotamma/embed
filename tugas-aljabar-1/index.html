SOAL 1

Diketahui matriks:

\[ A = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2 \\ 1 & 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}, \quad B = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \\ 3 & 2 \end{bmatrix}, \quad C = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix}, \quad D = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 2 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \]

a) Menghitung \( ABC \):

Langkah 1: Menghitung \( A \times B \)

\[ AB = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2 \\ 1 & 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \\ 3 & 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ 3 & -1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Langkah 2: Menghitung \( (AB) \times C \)

\[ ABC = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ 3 & -1 \end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -4 & 10 \\ -3 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \]

b) Menghitung \( C^2 - 2CD + D^2 \):

Menghitung \( C^2 \)

\[ C^2 = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \]

Menghitung \( 2CD \)

\[ 2CD = 2 \times \left(\begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 2 & 2 \end{bmatrix}\right) = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 8 & 8 \end{bmatrix} \]

Menghitung \( D^2 \)

\[ D^2 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 2 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 2 & 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 2 \\ 4 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \]

Hasil akhir \( C^2 - 2CD + D^2 \)

\[ C^2 - 2CD + D^2 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 8 & 8 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 2 \\ 4 & 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 0 \\ -4 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \]

SOAL 2

Menentukan nilai \( k \) sehingga \( P + kQ \) singular:

\[ P + kQ = \begin{bmatrix} 2 - k & 4 + 2k \\ 4 + k & 2 + k \end{bmatrix} \]

Dengan \( \text{det}(P + kQ) = 0 \):

\[ (2 - k)(2 + k) - (4 + 2k)(4 + k) = 0 \] \[ 4 + 2k - 2k - k^2 - (16 + 4k + 8k + 2k^2) = 0 \] \[ -3k^2 - 14k - 12 = 0 \]

Menggunakan rumus kuadrat:

\[ k = -6 \quad \text{atau} \quad k = -\frac{2}{3} \]

SOAL 4

Sistem Persamaan Linear (SPL):

\[ \begin{cases} x - y - z = 2 \\ x + 2y + z = 1 \\ x - z = 3 \end{cases} \]

a) Eliminasi Gauss

\[ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & -1 & | & 2 \\ 1 & 2 & 1 & | & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & -1 & | & 3 \end{bmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & -1 & | & 2 \\ 0 & 3 & 2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & | & 1 \end{bmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & -1 & | & 2 \\ 0 & 3 & 2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & -\frac{2}{3} & | & \frac{4}{3} \end{bmatrix} \]

b) Solusi

\[ z = -2, \quad y = 1, \quad x = 3 \]

c) Kesimpulan

SPL memiliki solusi tunggal: \( (x, y, z) = (3, 1, -2) \)

\[ \begin{cases} 2p + q - r + s = 3 \\ 3p - 2q + r + 2s = 5 \\ p - q + 2r + s = 1 \end{cases} \]

a) Eliminasi Gauss

\[ \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1 & -1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 3 & -2 & 1 & 2 & | & 5 \\ 1 & -1 & 2 & 1 & | & 1 \end{bmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1 & -1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & -3.5 & 2.5 & 0.5 & | & 2 \\ 0 & -1.5 & 2.5 & 0.5 & | & -0.5 \end{bmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1 & -1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1.07 & 0.79 & | & -1.14 \end{bmatrix} \]

b) Solusi parametrik (dengan parameter \( t \in \mathbb{R} \))

\[ p = 1.571 - 0.5t, \quad q = 0.857, \quad r = -1.14, \quad s = t \]

c) Kesimpulan

SPL memiliki tak hingga banyak solusi dengan parameter \( t \).
